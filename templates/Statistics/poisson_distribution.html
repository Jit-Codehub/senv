What do you Mean by Poisson Distribution

Poisson distribution is a separate probability distribution that describes the probability of an event occurring with a specified time interval. These events are assumed to take place for a known average rate of success or the mean of distribution and independent of the last event.

Poisson Distribution Formula

Let us see the Poisson distribution formula

P(X=x) = (e-λ * λx) / x!

Where,

x=0,1,2,3,..,n

P(X=x) is Poisson probability

λ= mean or average rate

e is the Euler's number (e= 2.71)

Here, λ and x be the non-negative integers.
