The method of measuring the ratio of mean and standard deviation is called coefficient of variation. Coefficient of variation is also called as relative standard deviation(RSD).

Coefficient of Variation Examples

Question 1: Find the coefficient of Variation for the sample 4, 3, 2, 5 of population?

Solution

Given set of values is 4, 3, 2, 5

Formula for Coefficient of variation

First, we need to find the mean and standard deviation

Mean

= (4+3+2+5)/4

= 14/4 = 3.5

Standard Deviation

= √((4- 3.5)2 + (3- 3.5)2 + (2- 3.5)2 + (5- 3.5)2)/(4-1)

= √((0.5)2 + (-0.5)2 + (-1.5)2 + (1.5)2)/3

= √((0.25)+(0.25)+(2.25)+(2.25))/3

= √(5/3)

= 1.29

Coefficient of Variation = 1.29/3.5 = 0.36

Thus, Coefficient of Variation is 0.36
