Sample Size Calculator with Example Solution

Example

Question: Find the sample size for the proportion of people shopping at supermarket. And in that vegan at 95% confidence interval and with 2% margin of error. Assume that proportion of population is 0.6 and infinite population.

Solution

Given, z = 95% confidence level, p=0.6

Z value taken from the table is 1.96 for 95% confidence.

Using the formula,

Infinite population (n) = [z2 x p(1-p)]/e2

n= (1.96)2 x 0.6 (1-0.6) / (0.02) 2

Sample size for infinite population is 2401
