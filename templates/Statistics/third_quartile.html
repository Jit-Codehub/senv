What does Upper or Third Quartile(Q3) Mean?

The middle term that lies between the median and the last term is called third quartile. It is denoted using the symbol Q3. On arranging set of observations in ascending order we have the third quartile formula as Third Quartile(Q3) = (3(n + 1)/4)th Term

How do you Interpret the Upper or Third Quartile Q3?

Go through the simple steps listed below to determine the Upper Half Quartile easily. They are as follows

  1. First and foremost step is to sort the given set of data in ascending order and split them into 4 equal quarters.
  2. Q1, Q2, and Q3 are the 3 quartiles and the third quartile is the upper half of the data.
  3. Sort the upper half in order and find out the median of the numbers.
  4. If at all the data is even, the third quartile of numbers becomes the middle value.
  5. In case if the data is odd, the upper quartile or Q3 of numbers is mean of two middle values.

Examples on How to find Third Quartile

Example 1: Find the upper quartile for the following set of numbers: 24, 16, 4, 6, 3, 5, 10, 2, 1

Solution:

Given Set of Numbers is 24, 16, 29, 4, 6, 3, 5, 10, 2, 1, 42

Sort the numbers in ascending order i.e. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 16, 24, 29, 42

We know the formula to find the upper quartile position is Q3 = 3/4(n+1)th Term

Here in this case n = 11

Q3 = 3/4(11+1)th Term

Q3=9th Term

Therefore, in the given set of numbers 9th term is 2
