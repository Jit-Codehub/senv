In statistics, the sample is the subset of the population. The mean determines the population mean. The sample mean is the average of n observations from the population. Also, it is defined as the measure of the center of the data or the sum of all observations divided by the number of observations. The symbol of the sample mean is x̄.

Sample Mean Formula

To solve the sample mean via calculators or by hand, we simply use the standard formula ie.,

Sample mean or Average (x̄) = (x1 + x2 + x3...+ xn) / n

x̄ = ( Σ xi ) / n

Where,
n is the total number of terms,
x1, x2, x3, . . . , xn (Σxi ) is the given n terms

Detailed Procedure To Calculate the Sample Mean

Finding the sample mean is so easy and simple as it adds up the number of items in a sample set and then divides that summation in the sample set. Following are the simple steps that show how to calculate the sample mean of a given data set:

  • First, consider all the given items and sum up the sample items'
  • Divide the added value by the number of samples
  • The obtained value is the sample mean of the given data set.

Example:

Find the sample mean of 25, 1, 7, 15, 6, 14?

Solution:

Given data is 25, 1, 7, 15, 6, 14

The formula to find the sample mean is (x1 + x2 + x3...+ xn) / n

= (25 + 1 + 7 + 15 + 6 + 14) / 6

= 68 / 6

= 11.33

Hence, the sample mean of the given data is 11.33.
