What do you mean by Covariance?

Covariance is exactly an interrelation between the two variables and also we check the relation is in which direction. If the two variables in same direction then they are positively related. otherwise, the two variables are in opposite direction that indicates that they are negatively related.

Covariance Formula

We can the Calculate the covaraince with the following formula.

Cov (X,Y) = Σ E((X-μ)E(Y-ν))/n

Where,

X and Y are random variables.

μ is Mean of the random variable X

v is the mean of random variable Y

n is the number of items in the data set.

= (-4)(-7.4) + (0)(-2.4) + (-1)(-2.4) + (1)(11.6) + (4)(1.6)
