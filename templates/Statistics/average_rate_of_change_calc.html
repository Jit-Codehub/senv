What is Average Rating?

Average Rating is a measure of the ratings given by the customers or people for a particular thing and finding the mean of all 1-5 star by calculating the total no.of votes. Let us see the formula for finding the average rating:

Average Rating = [(1 * p)+(2 * q)+(3 * r)+(4 * s)+(5 * t)]/N

Here,

p is the number of 1-star rating

q is the number of 2-star rating

r is the number of 3-star rating

s is the number of 4-star rating

t is the number of 5-star rating

N is the total number of votes

Average Rating = [(1 * p)+(2 * q)+(3 * r)+(4 * s)+(5 * t)]/N

AR = 23/7 = 3.28

Thus, the Average Rating is 3.28
