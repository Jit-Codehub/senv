What is Meant by Vote Percentage & Formula?

Finding the share of the vote for a participant in an election, political party, or election content is nothing but vote percentage. The formula for Vote Percentage Calculation is as follows:

C = V/T × 100

Where,

C is the percentage of the vote of a candidate
V is the number of votes for a candidate
T is the total number of votes

How to Calculate Polling Percentage in Election?

The steps included in calculating the polling votes percentage in an election are explained here clearly. Follow these guidelines and make your calculation much easier and faster manually:

  • Firstly, you have to calculate the total number of votes of each and every candidate who participated in the elections from all parties and other individuals.
  • Now, find the total number of votes polled by adding up all those values.
  • Once, you find those two numbers divide each candidate's votes by the total number of votes polled in the election.
  • Lastly, multiply the result by 100 to avail the percentages of the votes of all party candidates.

Solved Example on How to Calculate Percentage of a Vote

Example:

Find the percentage of the votes of all parties? If the number of votes polled in the election for the Democratic Party is 3,08,596, for Republican Party is 1,09,887 and for other parties is 4,00,968.

Solution:

Given that,

Numbers of votes polled for Democratic Party = 3,08,596

No of votes polled for Republican Party = 1,09,887

Numbers of votes polled for Other parties = 4,00,968

Now, find the total polled votes by summing up all the votes polled by each party

ie., 3,08,596 + 1,09,887 + 4,00,968

= 819451

Votes Percentage for Democratic Party = (Numbers of votes polled for Democratic Party / Total number of votes polled) x 100

= (3,08,596 / 819451) x 100

= 0.3765 x 100

= 37.65%

Votes Percentage for Republican Party = (Numbers of votes polled for Republican Party / Total number of votes polled) x 100

= (1,09,887 / 819451) x 100

= 0.1340 x 100

= 13.40%

Votes Percentage for Other parties = (Numbers of votes polled for Other parties / Total number of votes polled) x 100

= (4,00,968 / 819451) x 100

= 0.4893 x 100

= 48.93%

Hence, the Votes Percentage for Democratic Party is 37.65%, the Votes Percentage for Republican Party is 13.40%, Votes Percentage for the Other parties is 48.93%.
