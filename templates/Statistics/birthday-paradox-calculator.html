What is meant by Birthday Paradox?

Birthday's Paradox is a mathematical problem put forward by Von Mises for finding the value of N in a Problem. For instance in a group of N people there is a probability of 50% such that atleast 2 people share same birthdays. Thus N = 23 and so is the Paradox.

  • Raise the probability of 2 people not sharing a birthday to the power pairs i.e. P(B).

Since both of them are complementary events their probabilities sums up 1 so subtract P(B) from 1 i.e. P(B') = 1 - P(B)

FAQs on Birthday Probability

1. What is the Birthday Paradox Formula?

The Formula for determining the Birthday Paradox is p(n) = 1 - (365! / (365n x (365 - n)!))

2. What are the assumptions made to calculate the Birthday Probabilities?

Assumptions made in Birthday Probability Calculation is that birthdays are equally distributed over an year and leap years are ignored.
