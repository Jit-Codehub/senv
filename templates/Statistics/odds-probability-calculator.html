How to Find Odds Probability of Winning or Loosing?

The steps that you need to remember and follow while calculating probability is explanied below for your reference & quick understanding. Here are the basic and easy steps to find the result:

  • Firstly, check the problem to calculate the probability of winning or against winning.
  • Consider the chances for success and chances against winning from the questions as A and B.
  • The formula to find the odds probability is listed above.
  • PW = A / (A + B) is the formula to calculate the chance of winning probability.
  • PL = B / (A + B) is the formula to find the chance of losing probability.
  • Choose any of the formulas that suit your question and substitute the values in any of the above formulas.
  • Perform the denominator calculations by adding both and then the number to attain the winning or losing odd probability.

Odds Probability Formulas

Probability of winning = chances for success / all chances

PW = A / (A + B)

Probability of losing = chances against success / all chances

PL = B / (A + B)

All chances = chances for success + chances against success

Where A and B are the odds for the chance of winning and the chance of losing or change against winning.

Solution:

Pulling any other card you lose.

So, A=48, B=4

For 4 to 48 odds against winning;

Probability of Winning = (0.9231) or 92.3077%

Probability of Losing = (0.0769) or 7.6923%
